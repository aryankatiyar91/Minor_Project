You need a PC/mobile/lapy/tablet with good internet connectivity & Internet Explorer 5.0 or above.

No problem. You can access it from any smartphone having Internet connectivity.

6. Is it safe to login from Cyber Cafe?

Yes, provided you do not disclose your passwords to anyone and logout properly and take necessary precautions of login & logout.

You need to have an account at this online portal.

8.What if, I do not have an account with E-portal for farmers?

9.Can I change the User id and Password?

You can change your passwords any no. of times subsequently but you can not change your User Id.You have to register with new mobile number for new user id.

10.How to change passwords?

Passwords can be changed any time and any number of times To secure access to your account through Internet, it is recommended to click on Forget password & set a new password.

11. What happens if I forget my login password?

Nothing to worry. Just click on forgot password at the login page, fill the registered mobile number and submit.Than you able to set a new password.

12.What happens if I don't logout?

Please ensure to logout in proper way. However, if you abruptly close your account page your session will end. Kindly do not leave your systern in between login page, as this will give opportunity to others around you to operate your accounts.
